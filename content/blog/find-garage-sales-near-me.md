Saturday morning should be about treasure hunts, not dead-end drives. Here is how to find garage sales near you and show up informed.

## 1. Start with a searchable map

Old classified sites give you a title and a pin. Modern sale discovery lets you search inside listings — furniture, tools, baby gear, vinyl — before you leave the house.

## 2. Filter by date and distance

The best finds happen early. Look for sales happening today or this weekend, then sort by distance so your first stop is closest to home.

## 3. Build a route

Hitting three or four sales in one morning? Use a route planner to order stops by driving time instead of guessing. You will save gas and beat the crowds.

## 4. Set alerts

New sales pop up all week. Set a free alert for your ZIP code and favorite categories so you are first to know.

The best garage sale is one people actually show up to. Here is how to advertise yours online without spending a dollar.

## List with photos and items

Buyers skip vague listings. Add clear photos and list key items with prices so people know the trip is worth it.

## Share a link everywhere

Post your sale link in neighborhood Facebook groups, Nextdoor, and any local classifieds. The easier the link, the more clicks.

## Print a flyer + QR code

Tape a weatherproof flyer to a nearby corner or community board. A QR code lets drivers scan and preview while they are stopped at the light.

## Update live during the sale

Mark items sold, flash last-minute deals, and keep inventory current. Live updates bring back repeat browsers.

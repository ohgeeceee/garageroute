A great route is the difference between scoring three deals and wasting half a tank of gas. Here is how to plan yours.

## Pick your targets first

Add every sale that matches your wishlist. Look at photos, item categories, and verified seller badges to prioritize the most promising stops.

## Cluster by neighborhood

Group sales that are within a few miles of each other. One side of town per loop keeps driving low and browsing time high.

## Start early and end with backup stops

Begin with the sale you most want to hit. Add one or two late-morning backups in case an early stop is picked over.
